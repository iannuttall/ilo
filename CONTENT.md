# Content

The public site keeps ilo's existing search traffic while explaining the local-first product honestly.

## Rules

- Preserve published URLs unless a redirect is deliberate and tested.
- Keep tool pages useful without requiring signup.
- Keep blog posts focused on the reader's question.
- Do not claim a hosted report, account, plan, or remote MCP exists.
- Link product calls to the CLI docs, local MCP docs, GitHub repository, or sponsorship.
- Report pages are research guides until local analytics ships.
- Update sitemap and internal links when adding or removing a route.
- Avoid internal migration history in landing-page copy.
