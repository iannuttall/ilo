# Product

ilo is a local social publishing toolkit for people and AI agents.

## Principles

- Local before hosted.
- Agent-first without requiring an agent.
- One shared implementation behind the library, CLI, and MCP.
- Explicit confirmation before publication.
- Bring your own social provider credentials.
- Free and MIT licensed.

## Current scope

- Connect one X account with OAuth 2.0 PKCE.
- Create and list local drafts.
- Schedule drafts with ISO timestamps or plain language.
- Publish a post or draft after confirmation.
- Run the scheduler once or as a local watcher.
- Expose the same actions through local stdio MCP.
- Preserve ilo.so's public content, tools, and URLs.

## Next

- Stronger scheduler installation for launchd, systemd, and cron.
- Media uploads and threads.
- Bluesky provider.
- LinkedIn provider.
- Local imports and analytics inspired by the legacy ilo app.
- One-click Cloudflare deployment for the public site and tools.

## Not planned

- Accounts or workspaces.
- Subscriptions or usage credits.
- A hosted composer or dashboard.
- A hosted draft database.
- A first-party remote MCP service.
